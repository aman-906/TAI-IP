RED FORT

The Red Fort or Lal Qila is a historic fort in the Old Delhi , India. Emperor Shah Jahan commissioned construction of the Red Fort on 12 May 1639, when he decided to shift his capital from Agra to Delhi.

INDIA GATE

The India Gate is a war memorial located near the Kartavya path of New Delhi. It stands as a memorial to 84,000 soldiers of the Indian Army who died between 1914 and 1921 in the First World War.
